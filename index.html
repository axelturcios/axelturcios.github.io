BIOGRAPHY

Axel Turcios is a Honduran-American multimedia journalist, writer and blogger. Turcios was born in La Ceiba, Honduras and moved to Miami, FL when he was 13.


In 2013, he obtained an Associate in Arts degree in Mass Communications and Journalism from Miami Dade College. The same year he transferred to the University of Miami where he is currently majoring in Broadcast Journalism and minoring in Political Science.


In September 2012, he interned at Univision channel 23 in Miami, FL. In January 2013, he began a new internship at CBS’s affiliate in South Florida. In July of the same year, he started interning at America TeVe channel 41, an independent Spanish television station in Miami. A month later, he landed his first job as a news reporter and producer at the same station.


Turcios remained at America TeVe until December 2014 before leaving for a semester abroad in London, United Kingdom. There he attended the University of Westminster and describes the experience as “the best time of his life.” In late May of 2015, he returned to the United States looking for new opportunities. In July of the same year, he began working as an associate producer at Miami’s # 1 station, Telemundo 51.


Axel’s passion for journalism and travel has taken him to places he never thought about. While in Europe, he traveled to 13 countries. He wrote articles about his foreign experience and made friends he wants to keep forever.


Turcios’s main goal at the moment is to finish school and return to news reporting. He dreams of traveling the world to find interesting stories and share them with his unique style.


He feels grateful to God for having a family and friends who have supported him unconditionally from the beginning.
